    Optimal choice

    The Optimal Choice PEO service allows businesses of all sizes to transfer the responsibilities of a variety of components such as workers' compensation, payroll (including payroll tax filing), human resources, safety services and unemployment compensation administration. With Optimal Choice, employees of a business become "co-employed," meaning that Innovative Employer Solutions ultimately becomes the Administrative Employer while the business remains in control of aspects like the recruiting and hiring processes and focusing on revenue-generating activities.

    Key Advantages of the Professional Employer to help eliminate the “Red Tape”:

    ► Shared risk (we file your employees under our Federal ID number which allows us to administratively manage them for you and at the same time offer “group” benefits to them)

    ► You’re still in control (you still have all the operational control of your employees including schedules, rates, hiring & firing)
