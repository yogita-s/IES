New Business

What options does a new business owner have with regards to Workers Compensation Insurance, Human Resources Department or Workplace Safety?

Innovative Employer Solutions has been operating as a strategic partner with businesses, providing an affordable and efficient solution for managing the lifeline of their business - their employees. Organizing the administrative functions of a business such as payroll, payroll tax filing, human resources, workers compensation insurance, safety/risk management, and unemployment compensation are not only extremely time-consuming, but require a high level of experience and expertise necessary to avoid common legal pitfalls. Failure to adequately perform these functions leaves you open to audits, fines, penalties, and lawsuits. Effectively managing these back-office items while growing and improving their core business is a near impossible feat for most business owners. We can help.

Innovative Employer Solutions, through specific program design, helps companies increase their profitability by streamlining their administrative tasks, allowing them to concentrate on the creative part of their business.

We do this by offering several different options and service levels, allowing us to find the best solution tailored to your needs. Whether you need a full Professional Employer Solution, an ASO package providing many of those administrative tasks or just someone to process your payroll, we are your solution.
