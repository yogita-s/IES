Complete Solutions

 

IES is pleased to offer a host of benefits solutions and specialized services that keep businesses focused on doing business!

Our high-touch personal services include a host of advantages for businesses of all types, including streamlined payments for clients based on scheduled payroll, no yearly audits, and access to an intuitive online portal for employees - wherever business takes them.

The Optimal Choice Platform allows businesses of all sizes to be relieved of the responsibilities of workers' compensation, payroll (including payroll tax filing), human resources, safety services and unemployment compensation administration. Often referred to as "Co-employment," Innovative Employer Solutions becomes the Administrative Employer while the business remains in control and focused on revenue-generating activities.
