    Visit us at Business Fair 2011

    BUSINESS FAIR 2011 On Thursday, April 7, 2011, the Pennsuburban Chamber will host Business Fair 2011, Featuring a Taste of North Penn. This annual event will be held at Gwynedd-Mercy College, on Sumneytown Pike, in Gwynedd Valley. A kick-off membership luncheon will be held at the College, prior to the Expo. Guest speaker‐ Dave Schultz, former Philadelphia Flyer and Stanley Cup Champion -- Better Known As The Hammer. Admission to the Business Expo is free. The luncheon will be held at 11:30 a.m. cost $25.00 - followed by the Business Fair beginning at 1:30 p.m. and concluding at 5:00 p.m. -- after which the chamber will host a Business Card Exchange ending at 6:30 p.m. * Exhibitor's booth: $495 ($595 non-members) * Sponsorships available: $1475 includes booth. All sponsors receive extensive newspaper and promotional advertising, banner displayed at expo, recognition at the event and in Prospector, and a preference for booth location. Expo Motto ‐ Win With The Home Team A Prize Will Be Awarded to The Booth Having The Best Sports' Theme Kick Off Luncheon Guest Speaker: Dave The Hammer Shultz Don't miss this opportunity to network and display your company's services. Date: April 7, 2011 Time: 1:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. Location: Gwynedd-Mercy College 1325 Sumneytown Pike Gwynedd Valley, PA 19437 THERE IS NO NEED TO REGISTER FOR THE BUSINESS FAIR. HOWEVER, YOU MUST REGISTER FOR THE LUNCHEON AND WE WOULD ALSO APPRECIATE YOUR REGISTERING FOR THE BUSINESS CARD EXCHANGE - CONTINUE ON CALENDAR TO REGISTER. For additional information regarding booth reservations or sponsorships contact the chamber office (215) 362-9200 or info@pennsuburban.org.
