    Workers Compensation

    A Workers' Comp solution that works for any industry.

    From providing claim management to bill coordination and monitoring, Innovative Employer Solutions prides itself on offering detailed Workers' Comp programs that work for virtually all businesses.

    Workers' Compensation/Claims Administration*

    • Provide claims administration
    • Coordinate bills
    • Handle inquires
    • Maintain files
    • Monitor claims
    • Establish light duty and return to work programs
    • Assist in minimizing loss time exposure
    •  Investigate for potential fraudulent claims
    • Establish physician panels
    • Permit premium payment on a per payroll basis
    • Educate on how to look for "red flags"
    • Provide monthly customized reports including loss runs when requested

    Note: Services subject to eligibility requirements

    * IES is not a 3rd party administrator or licensed to adjust claims. Claims are adjusted and paid in accordance with the terms on the workers compensations insurace policy providing coverage. For more information please contact our customer service department.
